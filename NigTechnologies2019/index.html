    About Us

    Nigtech, an industry leader with an ever-strengthening clientele, brings cutting-edge and technically rich computer networking solutions, computer diagnose, New PC setup & security and a number of other similar solutions. We have in-house team of experienced and skilled software and hardware engineers who have got expertise in computer networking solutions and complete diagnose solutions. Right from network and system design, technology integration, network repair and maintenance, proactive services and support, system upgrades, network security and many more.

    Our Mission

    To preserve the functions of our physical assets throughout their technologically useful lives. To the satisfaction of their owners, of their users, and of society as a whole, by selecting and applying the most cost-effective techniques for managing failures and their consequences, With the active support of all the people involved.

    Our Plan

    Create a high level of availability through consistent operational safety work with a financial balance to achieve the production goals. Preventive maintenance is planned and based on condition-based maintenance. This is to minimize any unscheduled maintenance operations. Establish close cooperation between maintenance and operating staff.

    Our Vision

    To be the leading company in South Africa, to offer reliable service and excellent client experience.
